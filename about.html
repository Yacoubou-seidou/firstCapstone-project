NIGER CONNEXION
GLOBAL SUMMIT 2015

NIGER CONNEXION is an event agency whose niche is cultural entrepreneurship. Founded in 2009 and holder of a license issued by the APEIC (Agence de Promotion des Entreprises et Industries Culturelles), composed of a young and dynamic team always in search of performance and innovation. Associated with all the major cultural and artistic events in Niger since its creation, it is also equipped with modern equipment to match its performance.
